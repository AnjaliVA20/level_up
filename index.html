About Us

Level Up Pediatric and Women's Physiotherapy centre is a specialized clinic dedicated to empowering women and children through personalized physiotherapy, fitness, and wellness programs. We combine expert physiotherapy care, scientifically designed exercise programs, and a holistic approach to wellness.

Why You Should Choose Level Up?

Care that supports every small step towards better health and movement.

Child-Friendly Care

Fun, play-based physiotherapy to help children move, grow, and gain confidence.

Women’s Health Specialists

Expert care for pregnancy, post-delivery recovery, pelvic floor issues, and fitness.

Experienced Therapist

Qualified, caring physiotherapist who understand children and women’s needs.

Personalized Treatment

Each patient gets a treatment plan made specially for their condition and goals.

Our Services

Pediatric Physiotherapy and Early Intervention

Therapy for infants and children to improve movement, strength, balance, and development. Helps in achieving age-appropriate milestones.

Cerebral Palsy Rehabilitation

Specialized physiotherapy to improve mobility, posture, and daily functioning in children with cerebral palsy.

Developmental Delay & Neuro Rehabilitation

Programs to support children with delayed milestones in sitting, walking through guided exercises and play-based therapy.

Antenatal & Post Natal Physiotherapy

Safe exercises for before and after delivery to strengthen core muscles, improve posture, and support recovery.

Pelvic floor & core strengthening

Exercises and therapy to manage pelvic pain, urinary incontinence, and strengthen pelvic floor muscles.

Women's Fitness & Wellness Programs

Personalized fitness and physiotherapy programs focusing on strength, flexibility, posture, and overall wellness for women.

Pain Management

Relief and recovery programs for neck, back, joint, or muscle pain through exercise and physiotherapy techniques.

Musculoskeletal & Sports Injury Rehabilitation

We help you recover from body pain, injuries, or surgery. Our treatment helps reduce pain, improve movement, and make you strong again.

Posture correction & Ergonomic Training

Assessment and correction of posture to prevent pain, improve alignment, and enhance confidence.

Our Mission

To create a healthier, stronger community of women and children by promoting movement, strength, and confidence through physiotherapy and fitness.

Our Vision

To provide comprehensive, client-centered care that focuses on recovery, prevention, and long-term wellness.
